# Dashboard Brief

## Purpose

The dashboard is built as an executive review layer for supplier performance. It combines commercial scale, cost conversion, inventory exposure, and vendor ranking so leadership can act faster on the relationships that matter most.

## Core KPIs

- **Total Revenue**
- **Total Procurement Cost**
- **Adjusted Profit**
- **Adjusted Profit Margin**
- **Inventory at Risk**
- **Active Vendor Count**

## Recommended Views

1. Full vendor scorecard ranked by adjusted profit or performance score
2. Cost vs revenue comparison for major suppliers
3. Inventory-at-risk watchlist
4. Category economics by classification
5. Margin-light, high-spend vendor review

## Management Takeaways

- Concentration risk is material and should be monitored proactively.
- Inventory exposure is large enough to justify tighter purchasing discipline.
- The strongest suppliers deserve deeper strategic attention, while weak-margin vendors need review or renegotiation.
- Category mix quality creates room for smarter allocation decisions.
